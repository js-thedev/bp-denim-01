Intentions Behind This Denim

There are more sophisticated ways to treat denim more professionally, but this guide was written to provide a minimal guidance on treating denim.

At the end of the day, it is just a piece of clothing. Every piece of clothing from Banana Pose is created with a hope that it will serve as a tool that will keep you comfortable, both physically and mentally, while you work on yourself to conquer your world. As such, treating your denim should not be something stressful and the process should be kept at its minimal.

There is nothing to be stressed about even if your denim get unusual stains or wears. It will just be a reflection of your journey with your denim, and your denim becomes a personal diary that only you can read what happened.

Wishing you a fun journey with your denim.
